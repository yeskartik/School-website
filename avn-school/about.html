About Our School

Established in 1970, A.V.N. Inter College has been a centre of academic excellence in Jethwara, Pratapgarh.

History

Founded with a mission to provide quality education to the region, the school has grown steadily since 1970.

Vision

To empower students to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

Mission

  • Provide inclusive and value-based education.
  • Develop critical thinking and creativity.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Compassion

Infrastructure

Smart classrooms, well-equipped labs, spacious library, playgrounds and more.

Timeline

  2. 2017 - Science Block inaugurated
  3. 2026 - English classes Started
