About Our College

Established in 1964, A.V.N. Inter College has been a centre of academic excellence in Jethwara, Pratapgarh.

We believe that quality education is the right of every child, not a privilege. Our college was established with a vision to provide excellent education to students from rural areas, ensuring that no student has to leave their village or place an unnecessary financial burden on their family to receive a better education.

Our Mission

  • To provide free, high-quality education to every deserving student.
  • To distribute free textbooks and study materials.
  • To help students receive government scholarships and other financial assistance.
  • To offer career guidance for higher education, competitive examinations, and future career opportunities.
  • To provide modern classrooms, experienced teachers, and a positive learning environment.
  • To encourage discipline, confidence, leadership, and moral values among students.

What We Believe

  • Every child has the potential to succeed when given the right opportunities.
  • Financial difficulties should never become a barrier to education.
  • Rural students deserve the same quality of education as students in the best urban schools.
  • Education has the power to transform families, communities, and society.

Our Growth

The greatest proof of our commitment is the success of our students. Year after year, our students have delivered excellent academic results, earned scholarships, and progressed to higher education with confidence. The trust placed in us by parents continues to grow, and with every new academic session, our institution moves forward with greater strength and determination.

Our growth is not measured only by the number of students who join us, but by the number of lives we help shape. Every success story inspires us to raise our standards and work even harder for the future of rural education.

Our Commitment

We are committed to creating an institution where every student receives quality education, equal opportunities, and the guidance needed to achieve their dreams. Our goal is to empower young minds with knowledge, values, and confidence so they can build a brighter future for themselves, their families, and the nation.

Timeline

  1. 1964 - School founded by Pandit Sukhpati Ram Upadhyay
  2. 2017 -Our college received official government approval to offer the Science stream, enabling students to pursue Science education within their own village. This milestone opened new opportunities for students aspiring to build careers in engineering, medicine, research, and other scientific fields.
  3. 2026 -English Medium classes were introduced to provide students with quality education, stronger communication skills, and better opportunities for higher education and competitive careers.
